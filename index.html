I am Chenyu Wang, a first-year PhD student in Electrical and Computer Engineering (ECE) at Boston University, co-advised by Prof. Wenchao Li and Prof. Kayhan Batmanghelich. My research focuses on the intersection of AI health and safety. I am currently interested in two research questions: how humans can incorporate knowledge and specifications into models, and how effective communication can be established between humans and models.

Prior to joining Boston University, I earned a Master’s degree in ECE from the University of Michigan, Ann Arbor, and a Bachelor’s degree from Hangzhou Dianzi University.
