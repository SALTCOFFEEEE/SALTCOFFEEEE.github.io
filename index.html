I am Chenyu Wang, a second-year PhD Candidate in ECE at Boston University, co-advised by Prof. Wenchao Li and Prof. Kayhan Batmanghelich. I am now focused on vision-language models, uncertainty quantification, AI reasoning, explainability, and their applications in the medical domain.

Prior to joining Boston University, I earned a Master’s degree in ECE from the University of Michigan, Ann Arbor, and a Bachelor’s degree from Hangzhou Dianzi University.
